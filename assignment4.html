Assignment4

This dataset presents job opportunities in Michigan, illustrating the annual job percentages for students with various educational backgrounds: less than higher education, higher education, and a bachelor's degree.

Liar Liar

Pre_Process:-

The data has been preprocessed, with the date originally formatted as month_year, which made it challenging to work with. I then reprocessed the dates and aggregated the data to obtain the annual sums.

Observations:

Bachelor's or above:
This remains relatively stable, with slight fluctuations over the years, showing a slight increasing trend towards 2023.
HS Graduates:
The value decreases from 2009 to around 2017, then fluctuates until 2023.
Less than HS:
There is a steady decline from 2009 to around 2015, followed by a slight increase and fluctuation until 2023.

This dashboard presents the job distribution for each degree type. The "Liar_Liar" chart displays the same job data for each degree, but with the y-axis starting at 0.05. While the lines appear identical, the data in the "Liar_Liar" chart is the same as in the "Portfolio_LineChart" above.
